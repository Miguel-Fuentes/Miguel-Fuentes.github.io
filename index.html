Bio

I am a Ph.D. Candidate at CICS, University of Massachusetts Amherst. My research applies probabilistic machine learning techniques to problems in Differential Privacy and AI for science. Some problems I have worked on include: private data synthesis, private query answering, and modeling bird migration. My advisor is Prof. Daniel Sheldon.
